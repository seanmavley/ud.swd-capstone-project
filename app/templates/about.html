About CodeBySide

Ever wanted to see how your code will be rewritten in another programming language?

CodeBySide allows you to learn from others and challenge yourself in solving interesting code snippets, converting the best of your code into your favorite programming language.

CodeBySide is open to all to edit and improve.

To send feedback or contact support, send email to hello@khophi.co.

Updates

Incremental updates are rolled out to CodeBySide at every 5 days intervals.
