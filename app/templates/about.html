Get started with CodeBySide

On the homepage, click on any of the listed recent CodeSides. Select the 'From' dropdown and choose language you wish to compare to the 'To' box on the right.

Switch the languages from the dropdown menus to see language change instantly!

To Add a new CodeSide, Login and visit the New page and follow the onscreen instructions!
